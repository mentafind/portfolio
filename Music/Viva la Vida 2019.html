Viva la Vida 2019

Background

I arranged this piece for a saxophone quartet I had formed with my friends in grade 8. For this piece, however, one of my friends decided to play the trumet.

We played this for seniors at a retirement home concert. Unfortunately, there is no recording available. However, in 2023, I rearranged this piece, for which there is a video of our performance. Check it out here.
