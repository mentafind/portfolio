Viva la Vida 2022

Background

Shortly after a few friends and I formed a saxophone ensemble at our high school, we realised that there was very little music that could match our exact instrumentation. Because of this, I took it upon myself to revamp an old arrangement of Viva la Vida I had made in 2019. It was nice being able to fill in many of the missing parts in my new arrangement as the old one only had four instruments.
