Need You

Background

This arrangement of Need You was written for my high school's saxophone ensemble. I used prior knowledge of arranging for saxophones to tailor each part to the musician playing it.

As this song is not very famous, there were very little resources that could help me with my arrangement. Almost the entirety of this piece was arranged by ear.
